Got Questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions

What is Enervia?
Enervia is a full-service renewable energy company dedicated to helping homes and businesses transition to clean, affordable power. We handle everything from energy audits to solar installation and smart energy product integration.
What are the benefits of using solar energy?
Solar energy dramatically reduces your electricity bills, lowers your carbon footprint, increases property value, and provides energy independence. With current incentives and tax credits, the return on investment is faster than ever.
Can I install solar panels in a regular home?
Absolutely! Most homes are excellent candidates for solar installation. Our team will conduct a free site assessment to evaluate your roof orientation, shading, and energy consumption to design the perfect system for your home.
How long does the installation process take?
A typical residential solar installation takes 1–3 days. The full process from consultation to activation, including permits, usually takes 4–8 weeks. Commercial projects vary based on scale and complexity.
Is there a warranty included?
Yes! All our solar panels come with a 25-year performance warranty, and our workmanship warranty covers installation for 10 years. We also offer extended service plans for full peace of mind.
